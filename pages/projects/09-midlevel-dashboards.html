OP-009 // ANALYTICS

Midlevel Dashboards

LIVE 2025
SQL Tableau Analytics Stakeholder Reporting
Overview

Operational dashboards designed to surface mid-funnel metrics for business stakeholders — closing the gap between high-level executive reporting and the granular telemetry visible only to engineers. Built to give product and operations teams a self-serve view into the metrics that actually drive decisions.

Problem

What Needed Solving

Reporting existed at two extremes: executive summaries with top-line KPIs, and raw data tables accessible only to engineers. Mid-level stakeholders — product managers, operations leads, and team managers — had no reliable way to answer operational questions without filing a data request or waiting on an analyst.

  • No self-serve visibility into mid-funnel conversion and drop-off.
  • Ad-hoc data requests created bottlenecks and delayed decisions.
  • Metrics definitions were inconsistent across teams.
Solution

How It Was Built

Dashboards were built on top of a curated semantic layer — metrics were defined once in SQL and exposed as clean, documented views. This ensured consistent definitions across all downstream consumers and made dashboard maintenance straightforward as underlying schemas changed.

Each dashboard was scoped to a specific team's decision surface: what questions do they ask weekly, what actions do those answers drive, and what level of granularity is actionable vs. noise. Design emphasized clarity over density — fewer metrics, better context.
